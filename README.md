# Karma Dog Training Operations Efficiency Dashboard

## Project Overview

This Tableau dashboard analyzes operational efficiency for a dog training business by evaluating trainer workload, cancellation behavior, service demand, revenue contribution, and training quality outcomes.

The goal was to identify inefficiencies, operational risks, and optimization opportunities to improve trainer allocation, reduce cancellations, and maximize revenue performance.

---

## Business Problem

As business volume scales, leadership needs visibility into trainer performance, service demand, and operational bottlenecks.

This dashboard was designed to answer:

- Which trainers are carrying the highest workload?
- Which trainers present the highest cancellation risk?
- Which services generate the highest demand and revenue?
- Does higher workload correlate with increased cancellations?
- Are top-performing trainers maintaining quality outcomes?

---

## Tools Used

- Tableau

## Dashboard KPIs

- Total Revenue: **$734,600**
- Total Sessions: **2,500**
- Cancellation Rate: **15.5%**
- Avg Outcome Score: **7.36**

---

## Dashboard Components

### 1. Trainer Workload & Revenue Contribution
Analyzes session distribution across trainers to identify workload concentration and potential over-reliance.

### 2. Cancellation Rate by Trainer
Identifies trainers with elevated cancellation rates to surface operational risk.

### 3. Service Demand & Revenue Contribution
Measures which service types generate the highest business demand and revenue concentration.

### 4. Trainer Workload vs Cancellation Risk
Multi-variable scatter plot testing whether increased trainer workload correlates with cancellation risk, while layering revenue and quality outcomes.

---

## Key Insights

- Sarah Mitchell handled the highest trainer workload, creating heavy operational dependency.
- Olivia Reed showed the highest cancellation rate, indicating the greatest operational risk.
- Private Training generated the strongest service demand and revenue concentration.
- Higher workload did not consistently result in stronger quality outcomes.
- Several high-volume trainers showed elevated cancellation rates, suggesting workload imbalance may impact operational efficiency.

---

## Business Recommendations

- Rebalance trainer allocation to reduce burnout and operational dependency.
- Investigate high-cancellation trainers for scheduling inefficiencies or client fit issues.
- Expand private training capacity to support the highest-performing revenue stream.
- Use top-performing trainers as internal benchmarks for coaching and process improvement.
- Monitor workload-to-quality ratios regularly to maintain client retention and performance consistency.
